MyACUVUE™ is a point accumulation rewards program where you are allowed to redeem and enjoy promotional rewards based on the accumulated points over a period of time. 

Upon enrollment, you will be provided: - 

Membership

  • For every SGD$1 spent, 10 points will be awarded 
  • Special Birthday gifts in form of rewards or points during your birthday month 
  • Invitations to MyACUVUE™ promotions or events

Under *VIP Membership

  • For every SGD$1 spent, 12 points will be awarded for all purchases made after achieving VIP membership status.
  • Special Birthday gifts in form of rewards or points during your birthday month Invitations to MyACUVUE™ VIP promotions or events

* Accumulate SGD$600 nett spend within a year and be upgraded to VIP Membership. 

Any unused MyACUVUE™ points will expire within 18 months.
